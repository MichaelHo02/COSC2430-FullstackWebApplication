About Us

InstaKilogram is a web service similar to Instagram. To test our market, we are developing the MVP (Minimum Viable Product).Think of it as a new way to share life's moments and memories with family and friends.We all have these little things in our lives that we want to capture and store for ourselves - from breakfast in that perfect cafe, the tree at Christmas, or your baby's first steps. InstaKilogram will allow you to upload a photo or video and attach some text for your loved ones on any device and any time.It all started when I wanted to send my sister an album of my life as I see it, but didn't know how. So we've created InstaKilogram!
